# 哈密顿量
$$
H = -J_x \sum_{x\text{-links}} \sigma_j^x \sigma_k^x
- J_y \sum_{y\text{-links}} \sigma_j^y \sigma_k^y
- J_z \sum_{z\text{-links}} \sigma_j^z \sigma_k^z$$
