[[自旋]]



$$
\sigma_x = \mqty(0&1\\1&0),
\quad \sigma_y = \mqty(0&-\text{i}\\\text{i}&0),
\quad \sigma_z = \mqty(1&0\\0&-1)
$$
$$\sigma_x^2 = \sigma_y^2 = \sigma_z^2 = \mqty(1&0\\0&1) = \mathbb{1}$$

$$\sigma_x \sigma_y \sigma_z = \mi \mqty(1&0\\0&1) = \mi$$
